# International Tax Planning

## When to Use
Use this skill when the user:
- Asks about moving the company to another country
- Asks about Estonia e-Residency, Ireland, Cyprus, Dubai, etc.
- Wants to compare tax jurisdictions
- Asks about tax implications of becoming a digital nomad
- Asks about holding structures or IP licensing
- Asks about exit tax or change of fiscal residence
- Mentions any country outside Spain for tax purposes

## CRITICAL FIRST CHECK — ALWAYS DO THIS FIRST

Before ANY discussion of international tax planning:

1. **Ask**: "¿Dónde viven actualmente los socios/administradores?"
2. **If all live in Spain**: IMMEDIATELY warn about Art. 8.1 LIS (sede de dirección efectiva)
3. **State clearly**: Constituting abroad while directing from Spain = the company is Spanish tax resident regardless
4. **Quantify the risk**: fraud penalties (150% + criminal liability if >120K€)

```

│   ├── Ley de Startups (15% IS)

│   ├── Portugal (IFICI): exención dividendos 10 años
│   ├── Dubai: 0% IRPF sobre dividendos
│   └── S.L. española sigue pagando IS en España
│
└── TODOS se mudan fuera → DESLOCALIZACIÓN COMPLETA
    ├── Exit tax (Art. 19.1 LIS) — PAGAR antes de salir
    ├── Nueva empresa en destino o traslado de la S.L.
    └── REQUIERE asesor humano para la operación
```

## Recommended Optimization Path (by phase)

### Phase 1 (0-12 months): Spain optimization
- Ley de Startups → 15% IS
- Document R&D expenses → deduction
- Nivelación reserve → deferral
- **Effective rate: 10-13%**

### Phase 2 (12-24 months): Evaluate if equity warrants action
- Patent Box if registrable IP
- If a partner wants to relocate → Portugal IFICI or similar

### Phase 3 (24+ months): Only if significant profits
- Holding structure (NL/LU) only if >€200K/year profit
- Full relocation only with professional human advisor

## Critical Rules
- NEVER recommend structures that constitute tax fraud (offshore shell without substance)
- ALWAYS mention exit tax when discussing company relocation
- ALWAYS distinguish between legitimate tax planning (elisión) and tax evasion (evasión)
- ALWAYS recommend human advisor for actual relocation operations
- Spain's Ley Startups at 15% + R&D deductions often beats "offshore" after costs
